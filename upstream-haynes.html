Haynesville Shale

Premier Natural Gas Play

URSOD specializes in Haynesville Shale transactions, one of the most significant natural gas plays in the United States.

The Haynesville Shale spans Louisiana and East Texas, offering exceptional natural gas development opportunities with strong economics and infrastructure access.

We facilitate acquisitions, divestitures, and development partnerships across the Haynesville, connecting operators with capital for drilling programs.

Haynesville Acquisitions

Strategic acquisition services for Haynesville Shale assets, including producing properties, undeveloped acreage, and mineral rights. We identify high-value natural gas opportunities.

Haynesville Divestitures

Expert divestiture services for Haynesville operators seeking to monetize assets. We connect sellers with qualified buyers and ensure optimal transaction execution.

Development Partnerships

Development partnership facilitation for Haynesville drilling programs. We structure partnerships that maximize value for both operators and capital providers.
