Kinder Morgan

Pipeline Infrastructure Services

URSOD facilitates transactions and partnerships involving Kinder Morgan pipeline infrastructure, one of North America's largest energy infrastructure companies.

Kinder Morgan operates an extensive network of pipelines and terminals, providing critical infrastructure for transporting natural gas, crude oil, and refined products.

We connect pipeline operators, shippers, and investors to optimize infrastructure utilization and facilitate strategic transactions.

Pipeline Capacity

Pipeline capacity optimization and brokerage services. We connect shippers with available pipeline capacity and negotiate favorable transportation agreements.

Infrastructure Acquisitions

Strategic acquisition services for pipeline and terminal assets. We identify high-value infrastructure opportunities and facilitate transactions for operators and investors.

Infrastructure Partnerships

Partnership facilitation for pipeline development and expansion projects. We structure partnerships that maximize value for all stakeholders.
