Lease Services

Oil & Gas Lease Transactions

URSOD facilitates oil & gas lease acquisitions, connecting landowners with exploration companies seeking development opportunities.

Our lease services include acquisition, negotiation, due diligence, and transaction management for oil & gas leases across major producing basins.

We work with both operators seeking leasehold positions and landowners looking to monetize their mineral estate through lease agreements.

Lease Acquisition

Strategic acquisition of oil & gas leases for operators and exploration companies. We identify prime lease opportunities and negotiate favorable terms for our clients.

Lease Brokerage

Expert brokerage services connecting landowners with qualified operators. We facilitate lease transactions that benefit both parties while ensuring proper documentation and compliance.

Lease Negotiation

Comprehensive lease negotiation services, including bonus payments, royalty rates, and lease terms. We ensure optimal deal structures for both lessors and lessees.
