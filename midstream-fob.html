Free On Board Trading Services

URSOD facilitates FOB (Free On Board) trading and logistics services for oil & gas products, connecting buyers and sellers in international and domestic markets.

FOB trading involves transactions where the seller delivers goods on board a vessel or at a specified location, with the buyer assuming responsibility for transportation and logistics thereafter.

We optimize supply chain operations and facilitate FOB transactions that maximize value for both buyers and sellers.

FOB Trading

Comprehensive FOB trading services for crude oil, refined products, and natural gas liquids. We connect buyers and sellers and facilitate transactions with optimal terms.

Logistics Optimization

Supply chain optimization services for FOB transactions. We coordinate transportation, storage, and delivery logistics to ensure efficient operations.

Market access services connecting producers with buyers in key markets. We leverage our network to identify optimal trading opportunities and partners.
