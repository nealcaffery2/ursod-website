Upstream Operations

Exploration & Production Services

URSOD specializes in upstream oil & gas operations, connecting buyers and sellers in exploration and production ventures across major basins.

Our upstream services encompass mineral rights, lease acquisitions, and basin-specific opportunities in the Permian, Haynesville, Eagle Ford, and Bakken formations.

We facilitate transactions that maximize value for both operators and investors in the upstream sector.
