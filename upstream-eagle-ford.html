Eagle Ford Shale

Premier South Texas Play

URSOD specializes in Eagle Ford Shale transactions, one of the most significant oil and gas plays in South Texas.

The Eagle Ford Shale spans across South Texas, offering exceptional opportunities in both oil and natural gas development with strong economics and established infrastructure.

We facilitate acquisitions, divestitures, and development partnerships across the Eagle Ford, connecting operators with capital for drilling programs.

Eagle Ford Acquisitions

Strategic acquisition services for Eagle Ford Shale assets, including producing properties, undeveloped acreage, and mineral rights. We identify high-value opportunities across the play.

Eagle Ford Divestitures

Expert divestiture services for Eagle Ford operators seeking to monetize assets. We connect sellers with qualified buyers and ensure optimal transaction execution.

Development Partnerships

Development partnership facilitation for Eagle Ford drilling programs. We structure partnerships that maximize value for both operators and capital providers.
