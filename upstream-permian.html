Permian Basin

Premier Oil & Gas Basin

URSOD specializes in Permian Basin transactions, one of the most prolific oil-producing regions in the United States.

The Permian Basin spans West Texas and Southeast New Mexico, offering exceptional opportunities in both the Delaware and Midland sub-basins.

We facilitate acquisitions, divestitures, and joint ventures across the Permian, connecting operators with capital and strategic partners.

Permian Acquisitions

Strategic acquisition services for Permian Basin assets, including producing properties, undeveloped acreage, and mineral rights. We identify high-value opportunities across the basin.

Permian Divestitures

Expert divestiture services for Permian operators seeking to monetize assets. We connect sellers with qualified buyers and ensure optimal transaction execution.

Permian Joint Ventures

Joint venture facilitation for Permian development projects. We structure partnerships that maximize value for both operators and capital providers.
