Office Real Estate

Commercial Office Acquisition & Investment

URSOD specializes in commercial office real estate transactions, connecting buyers and sellers in office property acquisitions and investments.

Our office services encompass Class A, B, and C office buildings, corporate campuses, and mixed-use developments across major metropolitan markets.

We facilitate transactions that maximize value for both investors and tenants in the commercial office sector.

Office Acquisitions

Strategic acquisition services for office properties, including single-tenant and multi-tenant buildings. We identify high-value office opportunities and facilitate transactions for investors and owner-users.

Office Divestitures

Expert divestiture services for office property owners seeking to monetize assets. We connect sellers with qualified buyers and ensure optimal transaction execution.

Office Investment

Investment advisory services for office real estate, including market analysis, due diligence, and portfolio optimization. We help investors identify and execute on value-add opportunities.
