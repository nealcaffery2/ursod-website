Capital Raising

Public Offerings & Private Placement Memorandums

URSOD facilitates capital raising through public offerings and private placement memorandums (PPMs) for oil & gas and commercial real estate ventures.

We work with companies seeking to raise capital through structured offerings, connecting them with qualified investors and ensuring compliance with regulatory requirements.

Our expertise spans both public market offerings and private placements, providing comprehensive support from initial structuring through final execution.

Public Offerings

Comprehensive support for public market offerings, including IPO preparation, regulatory compliance, and investor relations. We guide companies through the complex process of going public, ensuring all requirements are met while maximizing capital raised.

Private Placement Memorandums (PPM)

Expert development and structuring of private placement memorandums for oil & gas and commercial real estate projects. Our PPMs are tailored to meet regulatory standards while effectively communicating investment opportunities to accredited investors.

Investor Relations

Strategic investor relations support to connect issuers with qualified investors. We leverage our extensive network to identify and engage potential investors aligned with your project's objectives and risk profile.
